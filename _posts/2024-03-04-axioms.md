I Was thinking of many things today and something hit me in the shower. Really this realization was the importance of sources. Yeah Yeah Boooooring I hear a younger me reading this saying.
Ok, listen.

In mathematics how do we write a proof? From what I understand Euclid laid the basis of how we prove things in math. Being that since we have to start somewhere we take axioms, things we take as our truths and build
on these axioms to prove different things. If those axioms are true then the things we have used logic to build on and infer from those axioms must be true.

When you think about a topic and make an argument for why you are right, essentially you are doing the same thing. You do research to get axioms, then you build on them with logic and arive at a conclusion.

Books in my experience are usually good sources. It takes time to read, most people dont read almost at all, and really one book could be biased, so you should read many books on the same topic
from different points of view to see if one sticks out like a sore thumb as being biased.

I feel quite a large discomfort at how un-reliable social media is for anything. It's like the Mcdonalds of information. 

Please dear citizen of the world I urge you to examine your beliefs and axioms and where they came from.
